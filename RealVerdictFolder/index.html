FAQ

Common questions — tap to expand.

Does it upload my numbers anywhere?
No. Every calculation runs in your browser. Saved analyses stay in your device’s local storage — nothing is sent to a server.
Is this investment advice?
No. RealVerdict is a calculator and screening tool. The verdict labels (Strong Buy, Pass, etc.) are outputs of a financial model, not recommendations. Always verify inputs and consult a professional before acting.
What should I verify before trusting the numbers?
Rent comps for the market, actual property tax and insurance quotes, realistic capex and vacancy rates for the asset class, and your final financing terms.
Can I compare multiple properties?
Yes. Save each analysis, then use the Compare feature to view two deals side by side. All saves are local to your device.
Does it work offline?
Yes. Add it to your home screen (PWA) and it works without a connection. The only feature that requires internet is the optional live mortgage rate fetch.
